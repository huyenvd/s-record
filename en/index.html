S-Record: Effective Order Management

S-Record is software designed for sellers on e-commerce platforms like TikTok, Shopee, Lazada... to record videos for managing various types of outgoing, returned, and shipping orders easily and user-friendly.

Key Features

  • Order Management: Record detailed videos for each order, including product information, recipient, and order status.
  • Return Tracking: Easily manage and track returned orders, minimizing mistakes and losses.
  • Shipping Management: Assist in managing the process of delivering orders to couriers, making the shipping process smoother and more efficient.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed with a straightforward and easy-to-use interface, suitable for all users.
